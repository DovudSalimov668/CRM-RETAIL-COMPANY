Customer Documentation

Complete guide for using the Retail CRM customer portal

Getting Started

Registration

  1. Click "Register" or "Sign up" from the home page
  2. Fill in your information:
    • Username (unique identifier)
    • Email address
    • First and Last Name
    • Phone number
    • Company name (optional)
    • Password (at least 8 characters)
  3. Click "Create Account" to complete registration

Login

  1. Navigate to /login/ or use the "Customer Login" option
  2. Enter your username and password
  3. Use the eye icon to show/hide your password
  4. Click "Login" to access your portal

Customer Portal

After logging in, you'll see your personalized customer portal with:

  • Welcome Section: Personalized greeting with your name and profile picture
  • Statistics Cards:
    • Total Orders - Number of orders you've placed
    • Total Spent - Total amount spent on all orders
    • Status - Your current customer status (Active, VIP, etc.)
    • Active Deals - Number of ongoing deals or promotions
  • Quick Actions: Shortcuts to common tasks
  • Recent Orders: Your latest order history
  • Profile Summary: Your account information

Shopping & Products

Browse Products

  1. Click "Shop" in the navigation menu
  2. Browse available products with images and descriptions
  3. Use filters to find specific products
  4. Click on a product to view details

Product Details

  • View product images, description, and specifications
  • See pricing and availability
  • Check stock quantity
  • Select quantity and add to cart

Shopping Cart

  • View all items in your cart
  • Update quantities or remove items
  • See total price including taxes and shipping
  • Proceed to checkout when ready

Checkout & Orders

Checkout Process

  1. Review your cart items and quantities
  2. Enter or confirm shipping address
  3. Add any special notes or instructions
  4. Review order total (subtotal, tax, shipping)
  5. Click "Place Order" to complete purchase

Order History

  • View all your past orders
  • See order status (Pending, Processing, Shipped, Delivered)
  • Check payment status
  • View order details and items
  • Track order dates and totals

Order Details

  • View complete order information
  • See all items with quantities and prices
  • Check shipping address and tracking
  • View order notes and status updates

Profile Management

Edit Profile

  1. Click "Profile" or "Edit Profile" in the navigation
  2. Update your information:
    • First and Last Name
    • Email address
    • Phone number
    • Company name
    • Address and location
  3. Profile Picture:
    • Upload a profile picture (JPG, PNG formats)
    • If no picture is uploaded, an avatar will be automatically generated
    • Preview your current picture before saving
  4. Click "Save Changes" to update your profile

Loyalty & Rewards

  • Earn loyalty points on purchases
  • View your points balance and tier status
  • Track point transactions and history
  • Redeem points for rewards (if available)
  • Check your loyalty tier (Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum)

Support & Help

Support Tickets

  • Create support tickets for issues or questions
  • Track ticket status and responses
  • Communicate with support staff
  • View ticket history

Feedback

  • Submit feedback about products or services
  • Rate your experience
  • Help improve our services

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Contact support or use the password reset feature if available.

Can I cancel an order?

Orders can be cancelled if they haven't been shipped. Contact support for assistance.

How do I track my order?

View your order details page for tracking information and status updates.

What payment methods are accepted?

Payment methods vary. Check with support or the checkout page for available options.

How do I update my shipping address?

Edit your profile to update your address, or enter a different address during checkout.

Tips & Best Practices

  • Keep your profile information up to date for faster checkout
  • Upload a profile picture for a personalized experience
  • Check your order history regularly to track deliveries
  • Save items to your cart and complete checkout later
  • Use the search function to quickly find products
  • Contact support if you encounter any issues
  • Review product details before adding to cart
  • Check stock availability before placing large orders

Mobile Access

The customer portal is fully responsive and works on:

  • Desktop computers and laptops
  • Tablets
  • Mobile phones

All features are available on mobile devices with an optimized interface.
